In order to win the game, you must finish 2 levels. each level consists of 9 enemies that can be shot by firing arrows at them.

Every level can be played indefinitely, but level2 can only be played upon completing level1.

The player has three lives (upper right corner). each time an enemy arrow hits him/her, he/she will lose a life. when all three lives are lost, the respective level is lost. you should avoid enemy arrows in order to increase your chances to complete the level.

The player can move up, down, right, left or diagonally across the screen by using the arrow keys.

In order to fire, press space.

To change the player, click on it to access the menu.

To turn sound on/off, click on the speaker icon.
